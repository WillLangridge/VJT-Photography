About Me

Where it all Started

Looking through the lens all started off as a hobby in school, where i attended photography club learning my camera as a basic. However over the years I wanted to change my artistic eye, skills and knowledge from basic to professional, going from a hobby to a career I would love, helping others capturing the memories with my shutter button

Qualifications

  • I am studying with NCFE, in my second year in Level 2 Photography.
  • I am a voluntary photographer gaining experience with a local football club South Park FC.
  • In the future I continue to learn, gain experience and build my own style behind the camera, with my skills and settings.

My Interests

I can capture a beautiful landscape to the urban culture. However I do enjoy capturing the love in a family-new-born, the strength in a relationship and the bond between humans and animals. Every memory is unique and personal, my career path would be family shoots, weddings and child's photography.
